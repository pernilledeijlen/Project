How many babies are actually born in cities in Europe? As you can see in the graph on the right, which shows the amount of births per year for the European Union, less and less babies are born. (Hover over the graph to see the exact amount and the corresponding year). But what are the differences in capital cities of Europe? That's what this page is about. What are the differences and what might be important variables that influence the number of births. The data used is from the most recent possible years, 2008 - 2014.


The graph on the right is from the world bank. Click on the graph its title or on world bank to go to the source.
