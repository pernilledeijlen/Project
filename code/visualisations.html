Babies born in European capitals


map of Europe

The map shows from light to dark how many babies were born in capitals of European countries. Hover over the map to see exactly how many babies were born and which dot in the scatterplot belongs to that city. Click on a country and scroll down for more information. When hovering over the legend the corresponding countries in the map and scatterplot will light up.

scatterplot

Use the click buttons to choose which variable you want on the y axis. When hovering over a dot, you will see the exact amounts and the corresponding country on the map will light up! Click on a dot and scroll down for more information.
